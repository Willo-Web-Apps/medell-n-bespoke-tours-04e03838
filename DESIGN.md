# Design DNA — Primavera Journeys

## Visual Personality: organic-natural
## Layout: storytelling
## Typography: elegant-serif

### Color Palette
- Primary (Forest Green): #2D5A3D — `oklch(0.34 0.08 152)` — headers, nav, key text
- Accent (Golden Amber): #C8872A — `oklch(0.62 0.12 65)` — CTAs, highlights, accents
- Surface (Warm Cream): #FAF6EF — `oklch(0.98 0.012 85)` — dominant background
- Text Dark: #1E3328 — `oklch(0.22 0.06 152)` — deep green for body text

### Font Pairing
- Display: Cormorant Garamond — weight 300–700 (luxury editorial, elegant contrast)
- Body: Montserrat — weight 300–500 (clean, international, readable at all sizes)

### Unique Design Choices
1. **Warm cream (#FAF6EF) dominant throughout** — clear departure from competitors' cold-white-corporate or dark-party layouts; feels like high-end travel magazine paper
2. **Full-bleed immersive hero** with layered OKLCH gradient overlay (forest green + amber), elegant serif headline in cream, with organic grain texture — NOT a stock photo grid
3. **Three traveler archetype cards** (Solo / Couples / Groups) flow out of a narrative "Why Primavera" section with generous whitespace and organic shapes
4. **Pull quote section** with oversized Cormorant Garamond italic, near full-width, magazine editorial feel
5. **Organic SVG wave dividers** between sections echoing Colombian mountain landscapes instead of flat horizontal rules
